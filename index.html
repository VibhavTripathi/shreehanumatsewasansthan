Description

At श्री हनुमंत सेवा संस्थान, we are dedicated to serving and protecting the sacred cow while also uplifting the underprivileged to bring their lives into the mainstream of society.

About

We provide shelter, food, and medical care to abandoned, injured, and aged cows. Additionally, we offer empowerment programs aimed at uplifting marginalized individuals and communities. Through community outreach efforts, we promote compassion towards animals and support sustainable agricultural practices.
